POWER BI

SALES DASHBOARD

Problem: The business lacked a centralized, real-time view of sales, profit, order status, and customer behavior, making it slow to detect revenue declines or high cancellation rates.

Solution: Built a multi-page Power BI dashboard integrating sales, cost, profit, and customer data, with filters by product, payment channel, shipping method, region, and time period.

Result: Provided a visual tool that quickly identifies sales trends, high-performing products, customer behavior, and areas needing improvement, reducing analysis time and accelerating decision-making.

POWER BI

LOAN ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

Problem: Data from 2007–2014 was scattered, making it hard to track defaults, assess risk, and spot trends, slowing risk detection and delaying decisions.

Solution: Built an interactive Power BI dashboard combining key metrics with filters by grade, purpose, location, and verification level, plus visuals on loan status, trends, and state performance.

Result: Delivered a centralized, real-time view that sped up detection of high-risk segments and improved decision-making in managing defaults and optimizing strategies.
